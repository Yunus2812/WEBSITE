ABOUT UNIVERSITY

The Castleton University is one of the leading university in the world

The Castleton University has more than 20,000 students, from 140 countries.

Castleton is a world-leading centre of learning, teaching and research and
the oldest university in the English-speaking world.

Each college is independent and self-governing, having a charter approved
by the Privy Council, under which it is governed by a Head of House, elected
and appointed by the governing body directly. The governing body comprises
a number of Fellows, most of whom also hold University posts.
